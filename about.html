Meet The Owner

Meet Boris Inzhin, the visionary behind Supreme Charters. Having over three decades of experience in the music industry as a renowned DJ and producer, DJ Boris has always been driven by a passion for creating unforgettable experiences. However, after years of performing at top venues and events worldwide, he discovered a new passion- yachting. Now having nearly a decade in the industry, he brings the same attention and detail, commitment to excellence, and love of entertainment to Supreme Charters. With Supreme Charters DJ Boris has combined his two passions to create a truly unique experience that will leave you feeling like a VIP
